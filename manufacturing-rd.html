Manufacturing & R&D

From concept to certified drone production

Advanced Drone Manufacturing Facility

At Sacha Industries Training Center, our manufacturing and R&D division focuses on building high-performance, reliable, and mission-ready drones using industry-grade processes.

  • In-house drone design & prototyping
  • Precision assembly & calibration
  • Component-level quality testing
  • DGCA-ready & industry-compliant builds

R&D Focus Areas

Flight Control Systems

Research on flight stability, PID tuning, autonomous navigation, and fail-safe algorithms.

Power & Battery Optimization

Improving flight endurance through battery efficiency, power distribution, and thermal management.

Payload & Sensor Integration

Integration of RGB, thermal, multispectral cameras, LiDAR, and custom industrial payloads.

Testing & Validation

Every drone undergoes multi-stage testing before delivery to ensure performance, safety, and reliability in real-world operations.

  • Ground & motor stress testing
  • Flight endurance & stability tests
  • Environmental & vibration testing
  • Pre-certification compliance checks
